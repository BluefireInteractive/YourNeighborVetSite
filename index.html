About Your Neighbor Vet

YNV is a privately-owned and locally-operated Veterinary Relief Team that delivers customized relief services to veterinary hospitals across Washington State and Iowa. With a commitment to personalized care, we ensure that our services meet the unique needs of each clinic we partner with, allowing them to maintain seamless operations even during staffing shortages.

Our highly skilled and experienced team includes a diverse group of professionals, from Doctors of Veterinary Medicine (DVMs) to Licensed Veterinary Technicians (LVTs) and Veterinary Assistants (VAs). Each member of our team is dedicated to upholding the highest standards of veterinary care, ensuring that your patients receive the compassionate, top-tier treatment they deserve.
