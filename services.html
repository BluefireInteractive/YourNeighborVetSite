DVM SERVICES

With a track record of happy hospitals, Your Neighbor Vet's team is exactly who you need to help when your doctors are on vacation or medical leave, or while you are searching for a DVM to fill an open position.

General Practice
Relief Coverage

  • We hire only the best vets and ensure they are well-equipped to adapt to your clinic's typical recommendations and protocols, workflow, and team dynamics.
  • Whether it be scheduled appointments, walk-ins, urgent care, dentistry, surgery, reviewing prescriptions, or reporting on lab results, our veterinarians are prepared to do what your team, clients, and clinic needs.
  • Our Team of relief veterinarians is familiar with a wide variety of PIMS including EZVet, Cornerstone, AVImark, many proprietary PIMS, and more.
  • Respect for your staff, clients, patients, and your hospital is what we do. Our vets are team players and will always work in cooperation and collaboration with you.

Emergency and Urgent Care
Relief Coverage

  • Our Emergency and Urgent Care veterinarians are highly experienced and know what it takes to handle inpatients, outpatients, and critical care - all at the same time. We will never assign a veterinarian to your hospital that is inexperienced in these areas.
  • Our ER vets are prepared to do common surgical procedures needed in an emergency setting, such as laceration repairs, foreign body removals, and more.
  • Remaining calm in stressful situations, our veterinarians stay laser-focused on the patients while working with your team, clients, and hospital standards to create treatment plans that find common ground.
  • Let us know what is important to you and we will send the best match from our team to yours - for a day or even long-term coverage.

The Freedom of Relief Work with
Employee Benefits and Reliable Pay!

Your Neighbor Vet is a privately-owned and locally-operated Veterinary Relief Team providing tailored services to veterinary hospitals in Puget Sound. We operate with the best principles of a family business:

  • We genuinely care about each other and our clients. We are a family.
  • You will always have a direct line to the Owner - no multiple layers of management.
  • Your job satisfaction is of utmost importance, which means you only work when you want, where you want, and how much you want.
  • Our goal is to match you with an average of 2-8 hospitals to be able to provide you and the hospitals the right amount of consistency.
  • We have multiple pay structures available - what fits your needs is what matters.
  • Practice the type of medicine you want to practice - general practice, emergency, surgery, urgent care or a mix - you pick.
